Image Sizes for Insights
========================
Each insight has its own folder with a card image.

Image specs:
- card.png/card.jpg: 1200x630 (1.91:1 landscape) - for insight grid cards & social sharing

Folder structure:
src/content/insights/
├── {insight-slug}/
│   ├── index.md (frontmatter + content)
│   └── card.png or card.jpg
